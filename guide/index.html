Getting Started

Welcome to ShipEngine! Our easy-to-use REST API lets you manage all of your shipping needs without worrying about the complexities of different carrier APIs and protocols. We handle all the heavy lifting so you can focus on providing a first-class shipping experience for your customers at the best possible prices.

Each of ShipEngine's features can be used by itself or in conjunction with each other to build powerful shipping functionality into your application or service.

Shipping Labels for Every Major Carrier

(opens new window)

ShipEngine makes it easy to create shipping labels for any carrier (opens new window) and download them (opens new window) in a variety of file formats (opens new window). You can even customize labels with your own messages (opens new window) and images (opens new window).

Real-Time Package Tracking

(opens new window)

With ShipEngine you can get the current status of a package (opens new window) or subscribe to real-time tracking updates (opens new window) via webhooks. You can also create customized tracking pages (opens new window) with your own branding so your customers will always know where their package is.

Compare Shipping Costs Across Carriers

(opens new window)

Make sure you ship as cost-effectively as possible by comparing rates across carriers (opens new window) using the ShipEngine Rates API. Or if you don't know the full shipment details yet, then you can get rate estimates (opens new window) with limited address info.

Worldwide Address Validation

(opens new window)

ShipEngine supports address validation (opens new window) for virtually every country on Earth (opens new window), including the United States, Canada, Great Britain, Australia, Germany, France, Norway, Spain, Sweden, Israel, Italy, and over 160 others.
